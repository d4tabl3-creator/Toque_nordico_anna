— Anna
📍 Itinerary
  • Casablanca — Hassan II Mosque
  • Rabat — Mohammed V Mausoleum
  • Fez — Ancient medina and tanneries
  • Todra Gorge and kasbah route
  • Sahara Desert — Berber camp
  • Aït Ben Haddou (UNESCO)
  • Marrakech — Souks and gardens
🌟 Experiences
  • Sunset camel ride in the Sahara
  • Tea ceremony with Berber families
  • Cooking class: traditional tagines
  • Artisan workshop in Fez
  • Desert sunrise from camp
  • Traditional Moroccan hammam
ℹ️ Practical Info
  • Best time: Apr–May / Oct–Nov
  • Visa: Not required for most countries (90 days)
  • Vaccines: Hepatitis A/B recommended
  • Currency: Moroccan Dirham (MAD)
  • Climate: 77–86°F days, cool nights
  • Languages: Arabic, Berber, French

📍 Itinerary
  • Lima — Culinary capital and historic center
  • Cusco — Navel of the Inca world
  • Sacred Valley — Ollantaytambo and Pisac
  • Inca Trail or scenic train
  • Machu Picchu — Sunrise at the citadel
  • Lake Titicaca — Uros floating islands
  • Rainbow Mountain (optional)
🌟 Experiences
  • Guided visit to Machu Picchu
  • Food tour in Lima (ceviche, pisco sour)
  • Panoramic train through the Andes
  • Quechua communities in Sacred Valley
  • Lake Titicaca island navigation
  • Coca tea and Peruvian chocolate tasting
ℹ️ Practical Info
  • Best time: May–September (dry season)
  • Visa: Not required for most countries (90–183 days)
  • Vaccines: Hepatitis A/B, Yellow fever (Amazon)
  • Currency: Peruvian Sol (PEN)
  • Altitude: Cusco at 11,150 ft — acclimatization needed
  • Climate: Dry and sunny, cold nights at altitude
